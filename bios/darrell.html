Biography

I am a PRACTICING Real Estate Salesperson in the State of Hawaii and Nevada. In Honolulu, I am licensed at Coldwell Banker Pacific Properties. In Las Vegas, I am licensed with Xpand Realty and Property Management. I discovered early on in my Real Estate Career that I enjoyed helping clients purchase property in Las Vegas. Whether for Investing, Retiring or Relocating, each situation brings me great satisfaction in helping my fellow Islanders with their Las Vegas property quest. As a Part Time Las Vegas and Hawaii resident, I am constantly in tune with what goes on in both markets.

Utilizing my vast construction experience, I help my clients understand the inner works of a property. I also supplement this experience with training in the Senior Field. My Senior Real Estate Specialist Designation (SRES) means that I am trained, have experience, and understand my fellow Kupuna. My Coldwell Banker International Diamond Society Award means that in addition to being in the Top 15% of Independent Agents in their worldwide system, I also have the transaction experience to navigate my clients through whatever situations may arise. As a past Coldwell Banker Global Luxury Team Member, I have the training, understanding and most importantly the experience of dealing with Global Luxury Properties.

I enjoy spending family time whenever possible with my wife of 35 years as we split time between Honolulu and Las Vegas. I feel so blessed to be working and living in Honolulu and our Ninth Island, Las Vegas.
